About Us

WHO ARE LIVE MASTERS?

Live Masters is a company created by and for music- and entertainment lovers. We strive to make entertainment available for anyone, anywhere. As the biggest ticket retailer on the market at the moment, we offer you a wide variety of amusement, always.



Established in 2017, we've been aiming to bring people together through all forms of events. Whether you love music, comedy, drama, or just want a family friendly show to bring your kids to, we assure you you'll find what you're looking for at Event Masters.

FOUNDER & CEO

Bert Karlsson is a well known man in the music industry. With a 200 year long history of finding some of the most outstanding pop stars of Sweden, he decided to take the next step in 2017 and created Live Masters.


He wanted a place to collect all kinds of entertainment and make it easily accessible for anyone. With the help of close friends and his soon to be Live Masters pro team, his vision became true.


"This is the greatest thing I've ever created, truly. I'll never be able to build such a fantastic thing again. The amazing collaborations we get to make, and the joy of seeing people at shows and knowing I've helped them on their way to that is priceless."
